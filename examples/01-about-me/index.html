All About Frank

From Humble Beginnings

Frank was born in the Shire and wished for nothing more than a life of afternoon tea and crumpets, until one day a group of gnomes visited his house.

The gnomes took him on a strange adventure to MOUNT SKULLFIRE where he slayed a dragon using a brick in a sock.

International Superstardom

After the release of his hit debut album I Slew A Dragon With A Brick In A Sock, Frank achieved international stardom as an experimental jazz-rock soloist.
